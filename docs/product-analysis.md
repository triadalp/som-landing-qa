# Product Analysis

## Product

Serwis S.O.M. is a commercial landing page for an automotive service located in Poznań, Poland.

The website contains:
- information about the service
- contact information
- service descriptions
- owner information
- customer reviews
- contact form
- communication channels

---

## Target User

Main target users:
- car owners
- people looking for vehicle diagnostics or repair services
- local customers searching for automotive service in Poznań area

---

## Main Business Goal

Convert website visitors into potential customers through:
- contact form submission
- phone calls
- Telegram communication

---

## Secondary User Actions

Users can also:
- check business hours
- open map location
- read reviews
- reserve a visit
- contact service via email

---

## Critical Areas

Most important areas for testing:
- contact form
- call buttons
- Telegram links
- contact information
- mobile responsiveness
- navigation

---

## Main Risks

Critical business risks:
- form submission failure
- broken contact buttons
- incorrect contact information
- poor mobile usability
- inability to contact the service
- loss of potential customers
